Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

TypeLess is a browser extension that pastes your own text templates into web forms. It is designed to collect nothing and send nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. TypeLess has no account, no analytics, no telemetry, and makes no network requests. We never see your templates or your browsing.

Where your data lives

Your templates and settings are stored only in your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) on your own device. They are never synced or transmitted unless you explicitly export them to a file you control.

Permissions

  • storage — save your templates locally.
  • activeTab — deliver a paste only to the tab you triggered it from.
  • scripting — inject the content script on demand; no remote code is ever loaded.
  • host access (all sites) — so you can paste into a form on whatever site you are using. The script is passive and makes no network requests.

Third parties

None. There are no third-party libraries bundled, no CDNs, and no remote services. You can verify this: every file is plain JavaScript and the source is public.
